This is the primary ethics class that all CS (+ X) majors take. Course structure include in-person lectures, mandatory discussion section, a presentation, weekly article surveys, and exams.

## Topics Covered

Exact topics vary by semester. In general, you will learn various moral frameworks, and whether current events or actions with in the computing space are moral based on these frameworks.
